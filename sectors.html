    WHERE WE ARE

    Introducing Mypikin

    MyPikinProject is a diverse activity designed to build research-informed platforms through scientific inquiry to mapping children's development in low-income countries. This is important because we need to understand how children develop and use their cognitive skills within their environment before they can be classified as cognitively functional or dysfunctional.

    OUR VISION

    Making an impact

    We aim to achieve this breakthrough through science, intervention, community collaborations, and rigorous study of children's developmental trajectories in Africa to enable a promising future for every child.
